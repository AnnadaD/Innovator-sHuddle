Esha Chandalya

Growing up I was told that I am someone a little ahead of my age group. They said that I give good advice and that I emit an ‘elder sister’ energy. It didn’t occur to me that I really wanted to become an elder sister to people until the pandemic hit and I could see how unfavorably some people were affected. Pandemic was also the time where I somewhat ‘found myself’ (it’s a process honestly) and I realized I wanted to help people, make them feel seen, heard and understood. That is when I created ascenteen (uh- sen-teen), the mental health and personality development venture. We are a small group of people trying to write blogs and create some products to assist young people into creating their own beautiful life. Come join me in a conversation about life, mental health, wellness, spirituality, self help and more on instagram @ascenteen

Sai Athale

Take A Bake

During the lockdown I had a lot of free time and my brother gave me the idea to take my love of baking and start a business and I did it! I started Take a Bake in July 2020 and haven’t looked back since. I bake and sell different kinds of brownies, cakes, muffins, cheesecakes and pizookies. Starting Take a Bake has been one of the best decisions of my life. The satisfaction you get from doing your own thing independently and earning from it is something else entirely. I’m extremely grateful to my amazing customers and family for supporting me on this journey.

Teertha Kulkarni

TI.ART

You may have heard people painting on canvas or papers but ever heard about painting on walls? So that's what I do! yes you heard it right, i paint walls and that's my venture; which offers customized wall painting services. Being an artist for more than 15 years I never thought beyond painting on paper but lockdown changed the game for me when I gave my first shot at wall mural painting and from then on there was nothing that could stop me! The venture is focused on providing customized wall mural services(wall paintings) for cafes, homes and various corporate spaces. Wherein I paint illustrations on walls according to the client requirements, which mostly tries to depict the purpose of existence of that particular given space. Till date I have completed 4-5 wall projects and I am planning to hop on many more as the venture progresses. Do contact me for wall mural services.

Tejasvi Zutshi

Uff Teri Adaa

Uff Teri Adaa is a small scale imitation jewelry venture which aims to serve its customers with their choice of customization on a large scale. It started its journey in December, 2021 with an investment of Rs.3000. And today, not yet profitable but still running on the revenue it generates for itself. Currently, it's focusing on two cities- Jammu and Pune and has a goal to spread across many more cities. It has served 150+ customers as far as now. Though still making its audience aware of one of the most affordable products of this category through trade fairs and social media and soon going to launch our own website. Main focus of Uff Teri Adaa is to make superior quality imitation trendy jewelry at the most affordable rates. It started with 3 customers on 25th December, making its first sale and there is no looking back from then on. It makes sure to get it's customers fidaa on its products and make everyone say for you- UFF TERI ADAA
